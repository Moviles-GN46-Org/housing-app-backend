# Problem Statement

Finding suitable housing is one of the most stressful challenges for university students. The primary pain point is the high risk of scams and fraudulent listings that target vulnerable students relocating for their studies.
Beyond security concerns, students struggle with fragmented information scattered across informal social media groups, a lack of reliable reviews from previous tenants, the difficulty of finding compatible roommates, and the absence of search filters tailored specifically to student needs such as proximity to campus, flexible leases, and strict budgets.

# Proposed Solution

UniHousing is a centralized mobile application designed specifically for the university community that connects students with verified housing options and compatible roommates. The platform directly tackles the fear of scams by validating landlords and properties, supported by a robust review system based on real peer experiences.
It offers advanced filtering for student-specific needs and includes a roommate matching system based on lifestyle and habits. By leveraging context-aware features and a direct chat with safety alerts, the app provides a transparent, secure, and highly tailored housing search experience.

# Solution Scope & Feature Tiers

Features are prioritized into three tiers based on the 8-week development timeline.

Tier 1 — Core

- User Registration & Login
- Dual Role System
- Property CRUD
- Search & Filters
- Map + List View
- Direct Messaging
- Property Reviews
- Report Suspicious Listing
- Landlord Identity Verification

Tier 2 — Important

- Roommate Matching
- Visit Scheduling
- Analytics Event Tracking
- Analytics Dashboard
- Inactive Listing Expiration

Tier 3 — Nice to Have

- Offline Cached Visits
- Accessibility Optimizations
- Fraud Detection Heuristics

Dropped Features

- Boosted Listings / Payments
- Sentiment Analysis on Messages
- Auto-Scaling / Disaster Recovery
- Transaction-Based Analytics (VD Map Q13–14)
